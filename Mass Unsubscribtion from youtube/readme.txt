
#How To Unsubscribe From All YouTube Channels At Once
steps -
1. First, you must log in to your YouTube account (obviously).
2. Click on the “Subscriptions” tab to see all the subscriptions list.
3. Click on the “MANAGE” button in the top-right corner to manage your subscription lists.
4. You need to inspect the webpage by right-clicking on the empty area of the page and selecting the “Inspect” option
5. Go to the Console tab and scroll down to the bottom until you get the “>” symbol. It is here that you will execute the script.
6. Copy the java-script that will automate mass unsubscription of the YouTube channels.
